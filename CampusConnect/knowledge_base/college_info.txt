# Raghu Engineering College (REC) — Knowledge Base for AI Voice Assistant

## Overview
Raghu Engineering College (REC) is one of the leading private engineering institutions in Andhra Pradesh, located amidst a serene and pollution-free environment on the outskirts of Visakhapatnam. Established in 1997 under the Raghu Educational Society, the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, industry-relevant curriculum, and holistic student development over more than two decades.

## Location & How to Reach
- **Full Address:** Dakamarri, Bheemunipatnam Mandal, Visakhapatnam – 531 162, Andhra Pradesh
- **Distance:** Approximately 35–38 km from Visakhapatnam city centre
- **Phone:** 08933-225500 / 08933-225501
- **Email:** rec@raghuenggcollege.com
- **Website:** www.raghuenggcollege.com
- **EAMCET Code:** RGTS

**How to Reach:**
- By Road: APSRTC buses from Visakhapatnam RTC Complex to Bheemunipatnam. Auto-rickshaws and cabs from Bhimili to Dakamarri. NH 216 provides good connectivity.
- By Train: Deboard at Visakhapatnam Railway Station, take APSRTC bus towards Bheemunipatnam (Bhimili).
- By Air: Nearest airport is Visakhapatnam International Airport (VTZ), approximately 40–45 km from campus.
- College Transport: REC operates its own bus service from multiple Visakhapatnam city pick-up points including Dwaraka Nagar, Steel Plant, and MVP Colony.

## Hostels & Residential Facilities
- **Boys Hostel:** Separate boys hostel with furnished rooms (2/3/4 seater options), mess, laundry, 24/7 security, and Wi-Fi. Capacity: approximately 700 beds.
- **Girls Hostel:** Separate girls hostel with furnished rooms, mess, dedicated security personnel, and Wi-Fi. Capacity: approximately 450 beds.
- **Room Types:** Both AC and non-AC room categories are available.
- **Hostel Mess:** Hygienic mess serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

## Food & Canteen
- **College Canteen:** Two canteens are located on campus providing affordable and hygienic meals for day scholars and staff.
- **Canteen Timings:** Open from 09:00 AM to 09:00 PM.
- **Cuisine:** Serves a variety of Indian and Chinese cuisine including breakfast and lunch options.

## Key Campus Facilities
- **Library:** Well-equipped central library with a large collection of books, periodicals, journals, and digital resources.
- **Computer Centers:** Advanced computer labs with high-speed internet connectivity.
- **Specialized Labs:** Nano Technology Lab, Electro Lounge, Coding Lounge, Invention Studio, Advanced Manufacturing Lab.
- **Sports Complex:** Cricket ground, volleyball courts, basketball courts, badminton courts, football field, and indoor games facilities.
- **Gymnasium:** Well-equipped gym available for students and staff.
- **Medical Centre:** On-campus first-aid and basic medical facility with a tie-up with nearby hospitals.
- **Auditorium:** Large auditorium for annual day events, cultural events, and guest lectures.
- **Conference Hall:** Air-conditioned conference halls for seminars, faculty meetings, and workshops.
- **Wi-Fi Campus:** Campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity in hostels, labs, and all academic blocks.
- **Power Backup:** Diesel generators for uninterrupted power supply; solar panels are installed on campus.
- **CCTV Surveillance:** Campus-wide CCTV cameras for security.
- **ATM:** ATM facility available on campus or in the near vicinity.

## Training & Placement
- **Placement Cell:** Active Training & Placement Cell (TPC) connects students with top companies throughout the year.
- **Training Programs:** Year-round training in aptitude, soft skills, coding, domain knowledge, and mock interviews.
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL Technologies, Cognizant, Tech Mahindra, Accenture, Capgemini, Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte, IBM, Oracle, Zoho, BHEL, HPCL, NTPC, L&T, DRDO, ISRO (internships).

## Student Activities
- **NCC & NSS:** Active NCC and NSS units for community service and national development.
- **Student Chapters:** IEEE, CSI, ISTE, and various department-level student chapters.
- **Cultural & Technical Clubs:** Various cultural fests, technical competitions, and student clubs for holistic development.

## Admission Process
- **For B.Tech:** Qualify AP EAMCET → Register for Web Counselling → Attend Certificate Verification → Report to Allotted College.
- **Government Quota:** ~70% of seats filled via AP EAMCET Web Counselling.
- **Management Quota:** ~30% of seats filled via direct admissions. Contact the admissions cell directly.
- **Lateral Entry:** Available for diploma holders (direct entry into B.Tech 2nd year).
- **For MBA:** Admission through ICET (Integrated Common Entrance Test), AP.

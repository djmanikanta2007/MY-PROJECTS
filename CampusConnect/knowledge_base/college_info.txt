# GITAM University, Visakhapatnam Campus - Knowledge Base

## Overview
GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) University is a premier educational institution located in Gandhi Nagar, Rushikonda, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. The campus is spread over approximately 255 acres and is known for its modern academic and residential infrastructure. It overlooks the scenic Bay of Bengal, providing a beautiful and serene environment for students.

## Academic & Administrative Blocks
The campus features state-of-the-art infrastructure dedicated to various schools and departments:
- **Academic Blocks:** The campus houses seventeen (17) academic blocks dedicated to engineering, management, science, pharmacy, and architecture. Important ones include the core Engineering Blocks (such as ICT Bhavan, Sir Visvesvaraya Bhavan, and the Science Block).
- **Administrative Blocks:** There are two primary administrative buildings managing admissions, student affairs, and university operations.

## Key Places & Central Facilities
- **Knowledge Resource Center (KRC):** The central library is a massive, multi-story building acting as the primary hub for books, digital resources, and research materials.
- **Vennela Canteen & Food Courts:** "Vennela" is the main campus canteen serving a wide variety of meals and snacks. There are several other food courts and cafes across the campus for students to hang out.
- **Auditoriums & Convention Center:** The campus features multiple seminar halls, open-air theaters, and a large convention center capable of accommodating over 1,200 people for major events and fests (like the annual KALAKRITHI fest).
- **Sports & Recreation:** The university has a massive playground, an indoor stadium, tennis courts, a modern gymnasium, and spaces for various student clubs to operate.
- **Amenities:** The campus is fully equipped with medical facilities (GITAM Hospital), banking services (Union Bank of India), ATMs, and high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity across all blocks.

## Additional Info
- **Contact:** The campus is always open to student inquiries at the main Administrative Block.
- **Transportation:** The university provides an extensive fleet of buses covering almost all parts of Visakhapatnam for day scholars.
